Ultimate_Cheeseburger

Description

This cheeseburger recipe is a cheesy upgrade to a favorite American classic. A perfectly seasoned flame grilled patty topped with 4 melty cheeses and curly fries tucked into a fluffy bun. This Ultimate Cheeseburger is a meal in itselfâ€“perfect for baseball season!

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs 80/20 beef *each burger was .33 lbs
  • 1/2 cup red onion sliced thin
  • 8 ounces green leaf lettuce
  • 8 ounces Beefsteak tomato
  • 8 seeded hamburger buns
  • 1/3 cup thousand island dressing
  • salt, pepper to season patties
  • 9 ounces yellow American cheese
  • 9 ounces jalapeno jack cheese
  • 9 ounces swiss cheese
  • 1/3 cup nacho cheese
  • 16 ounces McCain Seasoned Curly Fries

Steps

  1. Press hamburger meat into 1/3 lb patties.
  2. Season all sides with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill burgers until clear juices run free or until your desired level of cooked.
  4. Bake McCain Seasoned Curly Fries until done.
  5. Line up buns and smear the top half with thousand island dressing.
  6. Line the bottom half with lettuce, and nacho cheese.
  7. Add the hot burgers to the lettuce with nacho cheese.
  8. Top each with one slice of each of the 3 cheeses.
  9. Add onion, and tomato.
  10. Add 2-3 McCain Seasoned Curly Fries to each.
  11. Cover with top half of the bun and serve warm with additional fries.
